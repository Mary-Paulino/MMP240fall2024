style="color:rgb(0, 0, 0) rgb(20, 1, 20)"> Chocolate Chip Cookies

This chocolate chip cookies recpie makes dozens of delicious cookies with crisp edges and chewy middles loaded with chocolate chips!

>

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons hot water
  • Â½ teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup chopped walnut

Directions

  1. 1. Gather your ingredients, making sure you butter is softened, and your egss are room temperture.
  2. 2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  3. 3. Cream together the butter,white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth
  4. 4. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla.
  5. 5.Dissolve baking soda in hot water.
  6. 6. Add to batter along with salt.
  7. 7. Stir in flour, chocolate chips, and nuts.
  8. 8. Drop by large spoonfuls onto ungreased pans.
  9. 9. Bake for about 10 minutes in the preheated oven, or until edges are nicely browned.
